Super Delicious Food Menu

Appetizers

  • Caprese Salad

    Fresh tomatoes, mozzarella, and basil drizzled with balsamic glaze.

  • Spring rolls

    Cabbage, carrots, and bell peppers, along with sometimes shrimp, chicken, or tofu, all wrapped in a thin rice paper wrapper

  • Bruschetta

    Grilled bread rubbed with garlic and topped with various toppings e.g; mixture of diced tomatoes, fresh basil, garlic, olive oil, and sometimes balsamic vinegar.

Main Courses

  • Grilled Salmon

    Salmon fillet seasoned and grilled to perfection, served with lemon butter sauce.

  • Chicken Alfredo

    Tender pieces of chicken breast served over a bed of fettuccine pasta and smothered in a creamy Alfredo sauce made from butter, heavy cream, and Parmesan cheese.

  • Vegetarian stir-fry

    Assortment of vegetables such as bell peppers, broccoli, carrots, and snap peas, stir-fried together in a flavorful sauce made from soy sauce, garlic, ginger, and sesame oil

Desserts

  • Chocolate Fondue

    Assorted fruits and marshmallows served with rich chocolate fondue.

  • Cheesecake

    Rich and creamy mixture of cream cheese, sugar, and eggs on top of a graham cracker or cookie crust.

  • Fruit tart

    buttery pastry crust filled with a creamy custard or pastry cream and topped with a colorful assortment of fresh fruit, such as strawberries, blueberries, kiwi, and peaches.
